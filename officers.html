P.C.A.R.A. Club Officers

 This is our most current list of our club officers. If anyone is interested in one of the open positions, or to be eligible in the future for a club officer position, please contact your regional coordinator to get more info as to how to get involved to be in a good standing. Soon our voting members will be determined by dues paid, but for now we do not require club dues, and have an open forum for voting. Once we establish a fair, honest and accountable treasury, we will most likely collect club dues as per our club constitution, and follow the rules set forth by it. This will give all due paying members a fair vote in our Anual club elections.
