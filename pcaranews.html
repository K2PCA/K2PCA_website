P.C.A.R.A. News Board

February 1nd 2020: We have been having regular nets on Echolink at K2RMF-R! Please see the Net Calendar for more information.

January 22nd 2019: We have replaced our Net Calendar with a Google calendar which is easier to not only read but can also set up reminders for folks for the nets you wish to partake in. We have also incorporated a new security feature for our Bulletin Board, as we have seen an increase in "robot" users.

November 29th 2018: We have incorporated an interactive Fourum/Bulletn board to our page, allowing users to register and partake is several interactive topics. The board requires users to register but will offer full access to the forums as well as private messaging.

October 30th 2018: We have started the process of creating our club website.  We have secured the domain K2PCA.ORG, a host server, and are currently building the site.  With help from our club secretary Kevin Maehrer, our club Webmaster Mark Khusid, and myself, some pages are still works in progress, but we are actively working to better the site and improve the content. Look for changes.
