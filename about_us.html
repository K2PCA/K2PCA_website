About P.C.A.R.A.


The Patriots Coalition Amateur Radio Association or (P.C.A.R.A.) started in early 2017. The club came to be as previously many like minded Ham operators in NYS started working together to improve our comm across the state and local region. We envisioned a one stop EmComm plan that could be spread across our state and used by anyone who wanted to have a solid game plan incase of a natural disaster, or event. Many people were getting their Technician license or better. Still facing issues with propagation, and equipment, we started to look at programs such as Zello, Echolink even WinLink to help us stay in touch while working on getting better EQ for RF. In late 2016 a group of guys from Western NY decided to try concentrating on our region and improve our RF reach, and it was working. While still holding tight to the plans that were established by the folks Downstate & LI NY, we branded ourselves as a club and created K2PCA - Patriots Coalition Amateur Radio Association, with the help of many General or better hams, using HF and Digital, and Echolink, we have grown into a national club with members from 40 states. We owe most of this to the dedication and drive of that original group from LI/NYC area and all their help to get us rolling in the right direction. Going forward we will continue to pursue that drive and goal, improve our HF reach, and continue to encourage new hams, and old to get involved.

The organizational structure of Patriots Coalition Amateur Radio Association is National/Regional.  We maintain a web presence and also Facebook Groups for National and 4 regions, Eastern, Central, Mountain, and Pacific.  These regions, as their names indicate, follow the 4 time zones that cross the contiguous 48 States.  For simplicity, we include Alaska and Hawaii in the Pacific Region.  The map below shows the regions and the States they include.  The brightly shaded States are the States in which there are PCARA members.  The lightly shaded States are those in which we do not have members at this time.  This map will of course change as our membership changes.

Our club officers, include President, Vice President, Secretary, Treasurer, Trustee, Training Coordinator, Eastern Coordinator, Central Coordinator, Mountain Coordinator, Pacific Coordinator, and the Executive Committee.  Our list of officers can be found under the Officers link to the left.
