How to join The Patriots Coalition Amateur Radio Association

There are currently two ways to join the club. We do have a Facebook presence and accept member requests there, if choosing to use Facebook, please reply to the posted questions to quickly expidite your member request. The Facebook page can be found here: https://www.facebook.com/groups/903456089781143/

Or please email us your member request to pcara.usa@outlook.com please include your:

  •  Name:

  • Call Sign (if have)

  • Home Address

  • TXT phone # (not required)

We do not require new members to be licensed operators as we encourage everyone interested in amateur radio to join. We have probational membership for those looking to get licensed and do have resourse for study materials, tutoring and testing for anyone looking to obtain a license and will do what we can to help you persue  a license.
